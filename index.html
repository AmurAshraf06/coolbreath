About Us

Businesses are focused on making money while services are focused on serving. We are proud to announce that our first introduction is not a business, our first introduction is service. Kool n Heat Koncepts Service as the name suggests is an HVAC company with its core commitment to provide Toronto homeowners with the best of the best heating, cooling & insulation services and that too at affordable rates. Our specialities are sales, installation, maintenance, diagnosis, repair, replacement and relocation of all sorts of home comfort heating & cooling appliances, including Furnaces, Water Heaters, Boilers, Air Conditioners, Humidifiers, Thermostats and Air Cleaners.
